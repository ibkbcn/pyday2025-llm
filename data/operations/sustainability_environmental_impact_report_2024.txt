EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

Coffee Shop Empire is committed to environmental stewardship and sustainable business practices. This comprehensive report outlines our environmental impact, sustainability initiatives, and future commitments across our global operations.

Key Achievements 2024:
- 28% reduction in carbon emissions per store
- 45% increase in renewable energy usage
- 67% reduction in single-use cup waste
- 89% of coffee sources certified sustainable
- 34% water usage reduction per store
