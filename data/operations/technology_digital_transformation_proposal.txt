EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

Coffee Shop Empire is at a critical inflection point where technology investment will determine our competitive positioning in the rapidly evolving digital landscape. This comprehensive transformation initiative will modernize our technology stack, enhance customer experience, optimize operations, and position us for sustainable growth.

Key Objectives:
- Increase mobile order penetration from 18% to 45%
- Reduce customer wait times by 35%
- Improve operational efficiency by 28%
- Enhance data-driven decision making capabilities
- Achieve industry leadership in digital innovation
