BENEFITS PACKAGE:

HEALTH INSURANCE:
Medical Plans:
- PPO Plan: Company pays 80%, employee pays 20%
  - Individual: $150/month employee contribution
  - Family: $450/month employee contribution
- HSA Plan: Company pays 85%, employee pays 15%
  - Individual: $100/month employee contribution
  - Family: $300/month employee contribution
- Company HSA Contribution: $1,200 individual, $2,400 family annually

Dental Insurance:
- Company pays 100% for employee
- Family coverage: $35/month employee contribution

Vision Insurance:
- Company pays 100% for employee
- Family coverage: $15/month employee contribution

RETIREMENT PLANS:
401(k) Plan:
- Company Match: 6% of eligible compensation
- Vesting Schedule: 3 years cliff vesting
- Employee Contribution Limit: Up to 25% of compensation
- Company Profit Sharing: 3% of eligible compensation

Pension Plan (Executive Level):
- Eligibility: Director level and above
- Benefit Formula: 1.5% of final average compensation x years of service
- Normal Retirement Age: 65
- Early Retirement: Age 55 with 10 years of service

PAID TIME OFF:
Vacation Accrual:
- 0-2 years: 2 weeks (80 hours)
- 3-5 years: 3 weeks (120 hours)
- 6-10 years: 4 weeks (160 hours)
- 11-15 years: 5 weeks (200 hours)
- 16+ years: 6 weeks (240 hours)

Sick Leave:
- 8 days (64 hours) annually, accruing monthly
- Unlimited sick leave for serious illness with doctor's note

Holidays:
- 10 company-paid holidays annually
- Floating holidays: 2 additional days

Parental Leave:
- Maternity Leave: 12 weeks paid
- Paternity Leave: 4 weeks paid
- Adoption Leave: 12 weeks paid
